Description

Couscous is one of the most popular and beloved dishes in the world, particularly in the Maghreb countries such as Morocco It is often served at special occasions like weddings and religious holidays. But where does couscous come from , what are its variations, and how is it prepared? In this article, we'll explore these questions and much more.

Ingredients :

  • 500g of couscous
  • 1kg of lamb or chicken meat
  • 3 chopped onions
  • 4 carrots, sliced ​​into rounds
  • 4 courgettes, sliced ​​into rounds
  • 00g of cooked chickpeas
  • 3 tablespoons of ras el hanout
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • Olive oil
  • Water

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onions in olive oil until translucent.
  2. Add the meat and brown it on all sides.
  3. Add the spices and mix well so that the meat is well coated.
  4. Add the carrots and courgettes, along with the chickpeas, and mix well.
  5. Add enough water to cover all the ingredients, and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat and simmer for about 45 minutes to an hour, until the meat is tender.
  7. Meanwhile, prepare the couscous according to the package instructions.
  8. Once the meat is tender, remove it from the pot and set it aside.
  9. Add the couscous to the pot and mix well with the vegetables and cooking broth.
  10. Place the meat on top of the couscous and serve hot.
